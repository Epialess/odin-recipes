Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Description

Sweet chicken wings made with onions, honey, and garlic!

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion, chopped
  • ½ cup honey
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons mustard powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 5 pounds chicken wings
